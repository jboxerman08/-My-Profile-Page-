Personal Projects

Blender / 3D Projects

In 2020, after discovering Blender, I developed an interest in 3D and embarked on the creative learning journey. After working through the infamous Blender Guru Donut tutorial, I used the newly acquired knowledge and put it to the test.

The idea came to mind to recreate a simple logo I liked from a podcast I have been listening to, the Holy Ghost Notes Podcast, since its inception in 2019.

I soon after made the plunge to sharpen my blender skills and purchased a course through Polygun Runway, where most of the images in the gallery were made thanks to his course.

Following the course, I continued to develop my skills and created various different pieces, from the Christmas August Burns Red stage snowmen, the alien-like characters and UFO inspired by League of Legend's Bard, Kratos' axe (Leviathan Axe) and a 3D diorama of a New Holland Coffee Shop in Lancaster, PA.
