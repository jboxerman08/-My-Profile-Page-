Matt Greiner Lessons Booking App

May 2024 - Present

In early May 2024, the opportunity to design and build a responsive fullstack web app came up for a friend and drum instructor.

Being a drum instructor and touring musician, he needed a custom booking service tailored to his needs for his educational drumming website, Matt Greiner Lessons, that could handle bookings for 1 on 1 Zoom drum lessons, but also with the additional capabilities of toggling between booking forms for on-tour in person lessons for up to 5 students at a time per location.

After trying multiple out-of-the-box booking services but annoyed that none were customizable to fit his particular needs, I offered to design and build him a booking app from scratch, with the intention of learning an entirely new tech stack - a combination of Next.js + NextAuth, React + Typescript, and MongoDB, with Stripe integration for payment, hosted on Vercel.

Personal Projects

Blender / 3D Projects

In 2020, after discovering Blender, I developed an interest in 3D and embarked on the creative learning journey. Like most 3D enthusiasts, the first tutorial I started learning with was Blender Guru's infamous Donut tutorial.

After working through most of it, I used the newly acquired knowledge and put it to the test. The idea came to mind to recreate a simple logo I liked from a podcast I have been listening to, the Holy Ghost Notes Podcast, since its inception in 2019.

I soon after made the plunge to sharpen my blender skills and purchased a course through Polygun Runway, where most of the images in the gallery were made thanks to his course.

Following the course, I continued to develop my skills and created various different pieces, from the Ibanez guitar render in Atrium Audio's studio and the "Funko-pop-esque" recreations of myself and friends, to the recreation of LND Skatepark, a 3D diorama of a New Holland Coffee Shop in Lancaster, PA., and more in between, along with my journey to learning animations, showcased on Instagram as Manless Skater.
